# Week 1 Notes

## Selected Papers

### 1. Coverage-based Greybox Fuzzing as Markov Chain (AFLFast)
Authors: Marcel Böhme, Van-Thuan Pham, Abhik Roychoudhury
Year: 2016

Reason Selected:
Foundational paper on coverage-guided fuzzing and AFLFast.

---

### 2. Directed Greybox Fuzzing (AFLGo)
Authors: Marcel Böhme, Van-Thuan Pham, Manh-Dung Nguyen, Abhik Roychoudhury
Year: 2017

Reason Selected:
Introduces directed fuzzing toward specific target locations.

---

### 3. Dissecting American Fuzzy Lop: A FuzzBench Evaluation
Authors: Andrea Fioraldi et al.
Year: 2023

Reason Selected:
Analyzes why AFL works and evaluates its major design choices.

---

### 4. Fuzz4All: Universal Fuzzing with Large Language Models
Authors: Chunqiu Steven Xia et al.
Year: 2024

Reason Selected:
Directly relevant to AI-guided fuzzing using LLMs.

---

### 5. Large Language Model Assisted Fuzzing of Matter IoT Devices (mGPTFuzz)
Authors: Xiaoyue Ma, Lannan Luo, Qiang Zeng
Year: 2024

Reason Selected:
Real-world application of LLM-assisted fuzzing that discovered many bugs and CVEs.
